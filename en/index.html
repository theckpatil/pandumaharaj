Shri. Pandurang Maharaj

✽ Name- Shri. Pandurang Mahadu Patil.
✽ Birth- 01-04-1939, Nagaon, Dhule.
✽ Death- 03-02-2019 ( 80 Years )
✽ Samadhi- Shree Gurudatta Temple Nagaon, Dhule.

✽ More Information ✽

✽ Life Journey ✽

   Shri Pandurang Mahadu Patil, also known as "Pandu Maharaj" was born on April 1, 1939, in the Bhadane family of Nagaon village in Dhule District . Despite humble beginnings, he started working as a farmer from a young age. Through relentless hard work and dedication, he faced the initial struggles of his life.
   During his childhood, he was deeply influenced by spiritual surroundings both at home and in the community. Alongside his family, Maharaj engaged in agricultural work, contributing to his village. Despite their impoverished circumstances, he maintained steadfast faith in his beliefs and pursued spiritual practices.

✽ Spiritual Awakening ✽

   One day while working in the fields, Maharaj had a divine vision of Guru Gorakhnath (one of the Navnaths). This encounter marked the beginning of a new chapter in his life. Accepting the teachings of the Navnaths, he guided his devotees on their spiritual path.
   Under the guidance of Guru Shri Sant Kashivishwanath Baba (Aurangabad institution), Maharaj gained spiritual knowledge and progressed in his spiritual practices. Following Kashivishwanath Baba's instructions, Maharaj and his companions tirelessly worked to build the Shri Guru Dattatreya (Ek Mukhi Datt) temple in Nagaon. This temple became a significant religious center for the village, hosting numerous spiritual programs and fostering spiritual growth among devotees.

✽ Service and Devotion ✽

   Throughout his life, Maharaj dedicated much of his time to social service and spiritual activities. He guided many devotees and awakened their faith and devotion. Maharaj's unwavering efforts fostered unity and spiritual advancement among the people of Nagaon. He organized various religious programs in the village, which contributed to his popularity.
   Maharaj consistently promoted cooperation and unity among the villagers. He actively participated in various social and religious programs, fostering unity and solidarity. His efforts helped the people of Nagaon overcome differences and work together collaboratively.

✽ Mahasamadhi and Memorial ✽

   At the age of 80, on February 3, 2019, at approximately 6:45 AM, Maharaj attained Mahasamadhi. His samadhi is located on the upper side of the Shri Guru Dattatreya temple in Nagaon. The structure of the samadhi is aesthetically appealing, with a 6-feet long and 3-feet wide construction. The upper part houses a 2-feet statue of Shri Pandurang Maharaj and his padukas (footwear). The lower part accommodates the installation of Shri Maheshwar Aatmaling (Shivalinga) and Nandi. The construction of the samadhi is delicate and artistic, making it an attractive site.

✽ Teachings ✽

   Maharaj emphasized the importance of a pure mind, righteousness, and compassion to his devotees. Through his teachings and actions, he continually inspired his followers. Even today, Maharaj's blessings and teachings enlighten the lives of many devotees, guiding them on the path of truth, righteousness, compassion, and devotion.

✽ Conclusion ✽

   Maharaj's teachings have led many devotees to discover the paths of truth and righteousness in their lives. Through his discourses, he instilled in his followers the values of purity of mind, righteousness, compassion, and devotion. Maharaj's blessings and teachings continue to illuminate the lives of numerous devotees, serving as a guiding light.
